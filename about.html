About Us | RPG Character Tracker
AD SPACE

RPG Character Tracker was an idea sparked after hearing many complains from Roleplayers about the lack of simple, online character trackers.

While there are many Roleplay Tools out there on the internet, there are not many that allow multiple characters and are free to use. So, people who mun multiple characters have few resources to track their characters all in one place. Here at RPGCT, we promise a free, simple way to save your characters... whether OCs, Muses, or Game Characters.

Updates to this site will come in time, so please feel free to check back regularly.

Why Use Us?

Have you ever found yourself making multiple accounts for online Roleplay Communities or for multiple Character Sheets? Want an online space to store and share your characters or show them off? Tired of copying-and-pasting your rule pages over multiple RP accounts and just wish there was one central place to link too? Then RPG Character Tracker may be for you! Here we offer you a free, and simple way to store information about all your characters to share or keep safe.
